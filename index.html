    JOSH OWENS

    HEAD CHEF

    Josh is the founder of Four Bean Soup. In 1987 Josh's love story with technology began with his Atari 800XL and his dual 5.25 inch floppy drives. He has come a long way since those days filled with acne cream and leg warmers. Josh has been cultivating his Rails skills since 2005, and is a Rails Core Contributor. He led his development team to win the 2007 Rails Rumble competition for the development of TastyPlanner. Josh is the Podcaster and Producer of WebPulp TV.
